### GeneralUtils
GeneralUtils has a few small but useful utilities such as getting a `VRRig` from a `Photon.Realtime.Player`.
Example:
```cs
    VRRig localRig = HoneyLib.Utils.GeneralUtils.RigOfPlayer(Photon.Pun.PhotonNetwork.LocalPlayer);
```
